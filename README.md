# Generate Image and Video using AI

This project demonstrates a simple workflow for generating **AI images and videos** using prompt engineering.

The process includes:

1. Generating images using **Nano Banana**
2. Using those images as **start and end frames**
3. Creating a **cinematic product video** using **Google Flow (powered by Google Neo)**.

---

# Tools Used

| Tool                         | Purpose                  |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| **Nano Banana**              | Image generation         |
| **Google Flow (Google Neo)** | Text-to-Video generation |

---

# Workflow Overview

The overall workflow followed in this project:

1. Write detailed prompts for image creation.
2. Generate multiple images representing different scenes.
3. Select appropriate images to represent the product story.
4. Use the images as **start and end frames** for the video.
5. Use **Google Flow Text-to-Video** to generate a cinematic product video.

---

# Prompt 1

```
A cinematic animated scene of a cute friendly honeybee flying through a colorful flower garden toward an open kitchen window during warm sunset. The garden is filled with vibrant flowers, green leaves, and soft evening sunlight. The honeybee gently flies above the flowers with smooth wing movement. The cozy kitchen inside the house glows warmly with soft yellow light. Wooden shelves with jars and kitchen utensils are visible in the background. | **A – Action**      | Bee flying toward the open kitchen window               |
```

# Video Generation using Google Flow

The final video was generated using **Google Flow powered by Google Neo**.

The process uses:

* **Text-to-Video generation**
* **Start Image**
* **End Image**
* **Cinematic video prompt**

---

# Video Prompt

```
Ultra-realistic cinematic product video of a premium honey jar labeled "THAENARUVI" placed on a rustic wooden kitchen counter near a sunlit window. The jar lid is slightly open and thick golden honey slowly drips from a honey dipper above the jar, forming a glossy droplet that stretches and falls naturally.

Warm golden sunlight streams through the kitchen window, illuminating the translucent amber honey so it glows like liquid gold. Tiny reflections shimmer across the glass jar.

The honey droplet slowly elongates and falls into the jar, creating gentle ripples on the honey surface. A few small droplets slide down the jar rim naturally.

The camera performs a slow cinematic push-in toward the jar, keeping the label "THAENARUVI" sharp and readable throughout the shot.

The background shows a cozy rustic kitchen with wooden utensils, spice jars, and a copper pot softly blurred using shallow depth of field.

Motion remains smooth and stable. The jar, label, and environment remain consistent with no morphing or text changes.

Cinematic lighting, macro food photography style, ultra-realistic textures, warm golden atmosphere, premium advertisement look.
```

---

# Steps to Generate Video in Google Flow

1. Open **Google Flow**.
2. Navigate to **Text to Video Creation**.
3. Upload the **Start Image** (generated earlier).
4. Upload the **End Image**.
5. Paste the **Video Prompt**.
6. Adjust generation settings if required.
7. Click **Generate Video**.
8. Wait for the AI model to render the video.
9. Download the final generated video.

# Key Notes

* Use **clear and detailed prompts** for better results.
* Maintain **consistent objects and environment**.
* Avoid instructions that cause objects to **appear or disappear suddenly**.
* Describe **lighting, motion, and camera movement** clearly.
* Use **high-detail cinematic descriptions** for advertisement-quality visuals.
